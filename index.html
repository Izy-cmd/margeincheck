Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions.

How does MargeinCheck calculate profitability?
We combine supplier price, platform fees (Shopify, AliExpress), shipping costs, a customizable ad spend ratio, and your margin thresholds to calculate gross margin, net margin, and breakeven. The verdict (GO/PRUDENT/PASS) is then determined based on your thresholds.
What differentiates the plans?
The Discovery plan offers 5 free analyses with a 3-day history limit. The Pro plan unlocks unlimited analyses, a 12-month history, CSV/PDF export, and customizable thresholds. The Agency plan adds user accounts, full API access, and agency-specific features.
Is my data secure?
Yes, all data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We do not store any payment information. MargeinCheck is GDPR compliant and we do not share your data with any third parties.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription from your dashboard at any time. No commitments, no cancellation fees.
How can I export my analyses?
On the Pro and Agency plans, an export button is available in the history section. You can export as CSV (for Excel) or PDF (for your reports).
